Bigha se Gaj nikalne ka Formula

Gaj = Bigha × 3,025 (Pucca Bigha) ya Gaj = Bigha × 1,008.33 (Kaccha Bigha). Ye isliye, kyunki 1 Pucca Bigha = 27,225 sq ft hota hai aur 1 Gaj = 9 sq ft — to 27,225 ÷ 9 = 3,025 Gaj.

UP me Bigha aur Gaj ka Hisab Samjhein

Uttar Pradesh me zameen ki baat-cheet aaj bhi zyada tar Bigha me hoti hai, lekin registry, naksha pass aur plot ki buying-selling Gaj (Square Yard) ya square feet me hoti hai. Isliye Bigha to Gaj conversion UP ke property market ka sabse common sawaal hai.

Dhyan rahe — UP me Bigha do tarah ka hota hai. Pucca Bigha (3,025 Gaj) zyada tar western UP aur sarkari records me chalta hai, jabki kuch ilaqon ke purane kaagzaat me Kaccha Bigha (lagbhag 1,008 Gaj) likha hota hai. Dono me teen guna ka farak hai, isliye sauda karne se pehle ye confirm karna sabse zaroori hai ki kaun sa Bigha use ho raha hai.

UP me 1 Bigha = Kitne Gaj? (Quick Reference)

Seedha jawab — Uttar Pradesh me 1 Pucca Bigha = 3,025 Gaj. Common values:

  • 1 Bigha = 3,025 Gaj = 27,225 sq ft
  • 2 Bigha = 6,050 Gaj
  • 5 Bigha = 15,125 Gaj
  • 10 Bigha = 30,250 Gaj
  • Kaccha Bigha ke liye: 1 Bigha = 1,008.33 Gaj

Bigha, Biswa aur Gaj — Poora System

UP ka land system aapas me juda hai: 1 Bigha = 20 Biswa, aur 1 Pucca Biswa = 151.25 Gaj. Jab record me "1 Bigha 10 Biswa" likha ho, to iska matlab hai 1.5 Bigha = 4,537.5 Gaj. Khatauni aur khasra records me zameen aksar Bigha-Biswa me hoti hai, jabki plot ki buying-selling Gaj me — isliye ye conversion sabse common hai.

Pucca vs Kaccha Bigha — Teen Guna ka Farak

Western UP (Meerut, Saharanpur) aur sarkari records me zyada tar Pucca Bigha (3,025 Gaj) chalta hai. Lekin kuch ilaqon ke purane kaagzaat me Kaccha Bigha (sirf 1,008 Gaj) likha hota hai — dono me teen guna ka farak. Agar galti se Kaccha ko Pucca samajh liya, to zameen ki value teen guna galat aayegi. Isliye sauda se pehle confirm karein ki kaun sa Bigha likha hai.

Bigha ko Acre aur Square Feet me Badlein

Sirf Gaj hi nahi, Bigha doosri units me bhi badla jaata hai: Acre ke liye Bigha × 0.625 (1 Pucca Bigha = 0.625 Acre), aur square feet ke liye Bigha × 27,225. Misaal — 4 Bigha = 2.5 Acre = 1,08,900 sq ft.

Bigha ke aage ka hisab — Biswa to Square Feet (UP) aur Bigha to Acre bhi dekh sakte hain. Saare units ke liye main converter.

Frequently Asked Questions

UP me 1 Bigha me kitne Gaj hote hain?

UP me 1 Pucca Bigha = 3,025 Gaj aur 1 Kaccha Bigha = lagbhag 1,008 Gaj hota hai. Zyada tar sarkari records me Pucca Bigha use hota hai.

Pucca aur Kaccha Bigha me kya farak hai?

Pucca Bigha bada hota hai — 27,225 sq ft (3,025 Gaj). Kaccha Bigha uska kareeb ek-tihai hota hai — 9,075 sq ft (lagbhag 1,008 Gaj). Purane land records me dono use hote hain, isliye registry se pehle confirm karein ki kaun sa Bigha likha hai.

1 Gaj kitne square feet ka hota hai?

1 Gaj (Square Yard) = 9 square feet hota hai. Ye poore India me standard hai aur state ke hisaab se nahi badalta.

Kya UP ke har district me Bigha ki value same hoti hai?

Nahi, kuch districts aur tehsils me Bigha ki value thodi alag ho sakti hai. Yahan standard reference values di gayi hain — final sauda ya registry ke liye apne local tehsil/land record office se confirm karein.
