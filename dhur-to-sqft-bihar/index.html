Dhur se Square Feet nikalne ka Formula

Square Feet = Dhur × 68.06. Ye value Bihar ke system se aati hai: 1 Katha = 1,361.25 sq ft = 20 Dhur, to 1,361.25 ÷ 20 = 68.06 sq ft.

Dhur kahan use hota hai

Bihar aur eastern UP ke land records me zameen ka sabse chhota hissa Dhur me likha jaata hai — jaise "1 Katha 5 Dhur". Jamabandi ya kewala padhte waqt Dhur ka sq ft me hisab aana zaroori hai, warna chhote hisse ki value samajh nahi aati.

Frequently Asked Questions

Bihar me 1 Dhur me kitne square feet hote hain?

Bihar me 1 Dhur = lagbhag 68.06 square feet hota hai. Ye 1 Katha (1,361.25 sq ft) ka 20wa hissa hai.

Kitne Dhur ka 1 Katha hota hai?

Bihar me 20 Dhur = 1 Katha hota hai, aur 400 Dhur = 1 Bigha.

1 Dhur me kitne Gaj hote hain?

1 Dhur = lagbhag 7.56 Gaj hota hai, kyunki 68.06 sq ft ko 9 se divide karne par 7.56 aata hai.

Kya Dhur ki value har jagah same hoti hai?

Bihar me standard reference 68.06 sq ft hai, lekin kuch districts aur eastern UP me Dhur ki value alag ho sakti hai. Final hisab ke liye apne anchal office ke records se confirm karein.
