Gaj se Plot Size Samajhna

Gaj sirf area batata hai, plot ka exact shape nahi. Ek hi 100 gaj (900 sq ft) plot square (30×30) bhi ho sakta hai aur lamba rectangle (45×20) bhi — area same rahega. Frequently Asked Questions

100 gaj plot kitne by kitne ka hota hai?

100 gaj = 900 square feet hota hai. Agar plot square (chorus) ho to lagbhag 30 feet x 30 feet ka hota hai. Rectangle ho to 45 x 20 feet ya 36 x 25 feet bhi ho sakta hai — area 900 sq ft hi rahega, bas length aur width alag hogi.

100 gaj me kitne square feet hote hain?

100 gaj = 900 square feet hote hain, kyunki 1 gaj = 9 square feet hota hai. Ye 83.61 square meter ke barabar bhi hai.

Kya gaj se plot ki length aur width pata chal sakti hai?

Gaj sirf area batata hai, exact length-width nahi. Ek hi area (jaise 100 gaj = 900 sq ft) ke kai shape ho sakte hain — square 30x30, ya rectangle 45x20. Actual dimension plot ki registry ya naksha me likhi hoti hai.

50 gaj aur 200 gaj plot me kitna farak hai?

50 gaj = 450 sq ft (chhota plot), aur 200 gaj = 1,800 sq ft (usse chaar guna bada). 100 gaj = 900 sq ft beech me aata hai. Jitne gaj utna bada plot.
