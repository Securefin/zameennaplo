Gaj se Square Feet nikalne ka Formula

Square Feet = Gaj × 9. Gaj asal me Square Yard hi hai — 1 yard = 3 feet, to 1 square yard = 3 × 3 = 9 square feet. Isliye ye conversion exact hai, approximate nahi.

Plot sizes Gaj me kyu boli jaati hain

Delhi-NCR, UP, Haryana, Rajasthan me residential plots ki size Gaj me chalti hai — "50 gaj ka makaan", "100 gaj ka plot", "200 gaj ki kothi". Builder aur naksha square feet me kaam karte hain, isliye Gaj ka 9 se multiply karna har property buyer ke kaam ka hisab hai.

Frequently Asked Questions

1 Gaj me kitne square feet hote hain?

1 Gaj = 9 square feet hota hai. Gaj ko Square Yard bhi kehte hain, aur ye value poore India me same hai.

100 Gaj me kitne square feet hote hain?

100 Gaj = 900 square feet hota hai, jo lagbhag 83.61 square meter ke barabar hai.

Kya Gaj aur Square Yard same hote hain?

Haan, Gaj aur Square Yard ek hi unit ke do naam hain. 1 Gaj = 1 Square Yard = 9 square feet.

Kya Gaj ki value state ke hisaab se badalti hai?

Nahi. Bigha ya Katha ki tarah Gaj regional unit nahi hai — ye standard unit hai aur har state me 1 Gaj = 9 square feet hi hota hai.
