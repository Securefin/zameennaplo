Kanal se Marla nikalne ka Formula

Marla = Kanal × 20. Square feet ke liye: Sq Ft = Kanal × 5,445. Aur acre ka hisab yaad rakhein — 8 Kanal = 1 Acre (43,560 sq ft).

Kanal kahan use hota hai

Punjab, Haryana, Himachal aur Jammu ke land records me badi zameen Kanal-Marla me likhi jaati hai — jaise "2 Kanal 5 Marla". Farmhouse, kothi ya agricultural land ke saude me Kanal hi main unit hota hai, jabki construction ka hisab square feet me chalta hai.

1 Kanal me Kitne Marle Hote Hain?

Sabse seedha jawab — 1 Kanal = 20 Marle (Marla ka bahuvachan "Marle" bola jaata hai). Ye Punjab, Haryana, Himachal aur Jammu — sab jagah same hai. Saath me ye bhi yaad rakhein:

  • 1 Kanal = 20 Marle = 5,445 square feet = 605 Gaj
  • 1 Marla = 272.25 square feet = 30.25 Gaj
  • 8 Kanal = 1 Acre (43,560 sq ft)
  • 2 Kanal = 40 Marle, 5 Kanal = 100 Marle, 8 Kanal = 160 Marle

Punjabi me log aksar "ik kanal ch kine marle hote ne" poochte hain — jawab wahi hai, 20 Marle. Iska ulta bhi — agar Marle se Kanal nikalna ho to Marle ko 20 se divide karo. Jaise 28 Marle = 1.4 Kanal, aur 8 Kanal = 160 Marle.

Haryana aur Punjab me Kanal-Marla Same Hai?

Haan — Punjab, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh aur Jammu-Kashmir, in sab states me 1 Kanal = 20 Marla = 5,445 sq ft standard hai. Ye "Pucca" ya bada Marla kehlata hai. Sirf kuch purane pahadi (hilly) records me "chhota Marla" milta hai jo thoda alag hota hai, par modern records aur registry me upar wali standard value hi chalti hai. Isliye Haryana ho ya Punjab, hisab ek jaisa rehta hai.

Kanal ko Square Feet, Gaj aur Acre me Badlein

Kanal-Marla purane land records me hota hai, par construction aur buying-selling square feet ya Gaj me hoti hai. Conversion:

  • Square Feet: Kanal × 5,445 (jaise 2 Kanal = 10,890 sq ft)
  • Gaj: Kanal × 605
  • Acre: Kanal ÷ 8 (kyunki 8 Kanal = 1 Acre)
  • Square Meter: Kanal × 505.86

Kanal kahan aur kyu Use Hota Hai

Punjab, Haryana, Himachal aur Jammu ke land records me badi zameen Kanal-Marla me likhi jaati hai — jaise "2 Kanal 5 Marla". Farmhouse, kothi ya kheti ki zameen ke saude me Kanal hi main unit hota hai. Jamabandi (record of rights) padhte waqt Kanal-Marla ka square feet me hisab aana zaroori hai, taaki zameen ki asli size samajh aaye. Misaal — "1 Kanal 10 Marle" ka matlab hai 30 Marle = 8,167.5 sq ft.
