Bihar me 1 Katha = Kitne Square Feet?

Seedha jawab — Bihar me 1 Katha = 1,361.25 square feet hota hai. Ye value 1 Bigha = 27,225 sq ft = 20 Katha ke hisaab se nikalti hai. Common conversions:

  • 1 Katha = 1,361.25 sq ft = 151.25 Gaj = 20 Dhur
  • 2 Katha = 2,722.5 sq ft
  • 5 Katha = 6,806.25 sq ft
  • 10 Katha = 13,612.5 sq ft
  • 20 Katha = 1 Bigha = 27,225 sq ft

Kya Bihar ke Har District me Katha Same Hota Hai?

Ye sabse zaroori baat hai. Yahan jo 1,361.25 sq ft di gayi hai wo standard reference hai, lekin Bihar me Katha ki value district-wise kaafi badalti hai. Patna, Gaya jaise ilaqon me ye value chalti hai, par Muzaffarpur, North Bihar ke kuch hisson me 1 Katha 1,361 sq ft ke bajaye alag (kahin ~1,742 sq ft, kahin chhota) bhi ho sakta hai. Katha, Dhur aur Bigha ka Relation

Bihar ka system ek chain me juda hai: 1 Bigha = 20 Katha, aur 1 Katha = 20 Dhur. Matlab 1 Bigha = 400 Dhur. Jab record me likha ho "5 Katha 10 Dhur", to iska matlab hai 5.5 Katha = 7,486.875 sq ft. Chhote hisse Dhur me, residential plot Katha me, aur badi zameen Bigha me boli jaati hai.
