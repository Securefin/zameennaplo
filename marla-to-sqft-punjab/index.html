Marla se Square Feet nikalne ka Formula

Square Feet = Marla × 272.25. Punjab-Haryana ka system kaafi standard hai: 1 Marla = 272.25 sq ft = 30.25 Gaj, aur 20 Marla milkar 1 Kanal (5,445 sq ft) banate hain.

Punjab-Haryana me Marla ka Hisab

Chandigarh, Ludhiana, Gurgaon, Karnal jaise ilaqon me residential plot ki size aam taur par Marla me boli jaati hai — "5 Marla ka makaan", "10 Marla ki kothi". Naksha pass karane aur construction cost nikalne ke liye Marla ka square feet me hisab sabse pehla step hai.

Punjab me 1 Marla = Kitne Square Feet?

Seedha jawab — Punjab aur Haryana me 1 Marla = 272.25 square feet (30.25 Gaj) hota hai. Common plot sizes:

  • 1 Marla = 272.25 sq ft
  • 5 Marla = 1,361.25 sq ft (sabse common ghar size)
  • 7 Marla = 1,905.75 sq ft
  • 10 Marla = 2,722.5 sq ft
  • 20 Marla = 1 Kanal = 5,445 sq ft

"5 Marla ka makaan" Punjab-Haryana me sabse common ghar hota hai — yani 1,361 sq ft ka plot.

Punjab me 1 Marla Kitne Foot ka Hota Hai?

Log aksar poochte hain "1 Marla kitne foot ka hota hai" — yaad rahe, Marla area ki unit hai, length nahi. 1 Marla = 272.25 square feet (area). Ye 9 marle ka system kehlata hai jisme 1 Marla = 272.25 sq ft hota hai. Agar koi square plot ho to 1 Marla lagbhag 16.5 ft × 16.5 ft ka hoga — par actual shape plot ke hisaab se alag hota hai.

Haryana aur Punjab me Marla Same Hai?

Haan — Punjab, Haryana, Himachal aur Chandigarh me 1 Marla = 272.25 sq ft standard (bada/Pucca Marla) chalta hai. Sirf kuch purane pahadi records me "chhota Marla" (~225 sq ft) milta hai, par registry aur modern records me upar wali value hi use hoti hai. Isliye Gurgaon ho ya Ludhiana, hisab same rehta hai.

Marla ko Gaj, Square Meter aur Kanal me Badlein

1 Marla = 30.25 Gaj = 25.29 square meter. Aur bade hisab ke liye — 20 Marla = 1 Kanal, 160 Marla = 1 Acre. Misaal: 8 Marla = 2,178 sq ft = 242 Gaj.
